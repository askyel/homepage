About

Hi, I'm Ariel. I'm currently studying Computer Science and Engineering at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I'm interested in data science, design, education, and how they intersect.

On campus, I'm an undergraduate researcher with the Collective Learning Group at the MIT Media Lab, working on an open source suite of data visualization tools for React Native. I'm also a project team member with Design for America, a counselor with Camp Kesem, an ultimate frisbee player with sMITe, and a member of Kappa Alpha Theta.

This summer I'll be a software enginnering intern at Rent the Runway. In the past I've worked as a machine learning research assistant at the Columbia University Center for Computational Learning Systems and a teaching assistant with Computer Science for Urban Youth.
